About The Author

Anna Barker: Anna is a journalist and published author of two novels, The Floating Island and Before I Knew Him (Random House). She is also an academic writing tutor and Royal Literary Fund Consultant Fellow. She developed ALEX while helping students develop their academic writing at Teesside University.

Royal Literary Fund: The Royal Literary Fund is a benevolent fund for professional published authors; it is funded exclusively by bequests and donations from writers and others who wish to help writers. The RLF also runs a Fellowship Scheme in partnership with universities. Writers work one-to-one with students, using their expertise in language and communication to help them develop their essay writing. www.rlf.org.uk

Teesside University: Design (Stephen Murray), App development, Asset Production and build (Andrew Bingham).
